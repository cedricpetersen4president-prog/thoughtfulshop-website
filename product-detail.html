Cordless Drill Set

High-performance 20V MAX cordless drill with two batteries and a complete accessory kit. Perfect for all your home DIY projects, from simple repairs to building furniture. Features a compact, lightweight design.

This comprehensive cordless drill set provides the power and versatility needed for any project. The high-performance 20V MAX motor delivers 300 unit watts out (UWO) of power, making it easy to drill through wood, plastic, and metal.
